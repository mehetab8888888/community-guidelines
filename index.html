Community Guidelines

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Our Mission

YAPPZY is a platform for creativity, expression, and connection. We're committed to building a safe, inclusive, and positive community where everyone feels welcome.

These Community Guidelines help us maintain a healthy environment for all users. By using YAPPZY, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

Safety First

Protecting Minors

We have zero tolerance for content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors. This includes:

  • Nudity or sexualized content involving anyone under 18
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Child endangerment or abuse

Violence & Threats

We do not allow content that promotes, glorifies, or incites violence. This includes:

  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Threats of violence against individuals or groups
  • Promotion of terrorism or violent extremism
  • Instructions for making weapons or explosives

Self-Harm & Suicide

Content that promotes self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders is prohibited. If you're struggling, please reach out for help.

Respect & Kindness

No Hate Speech

We do not tolerate hate speech or discrimination based on:

  • Race, ethnicity, or national origin
  • Religion or religious beliefs
  • Sexual orientation or gender identity
  • Disability or medical condition
  • Caste, age, or veteran status

No Harassment or Bullying

Treat others with respect. Do not engage in:

  • Targeted harassment or cyberbullying
  • Doxxing (sharing private information)
  • Stalking or unwanted attention
  • Impersonation

Sexual Content

Sexually explicit content, nudity, and pornography are not allowed. This includes:

  • Nudity or sexually suggestive content
  • Sexual acts or simulations
  • Sexual solicitation

Integrity & Authenticity

Misinformation

Do not spread false information that could cause harm, especially regarding:

  • Health and medical misinformation
  • Elections and civic processes
  • Manipulated media (deepfakes)

Spam & Fake Engagement

Authentic engagement only. Do not:

  • Use bots or fake accounts
  • Artificially inflate views, likes, or followers
  • Post repetitive or misleading content
  • Engage in follow-for-follow schemes

Scams & Fraud

Fraudulent activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Phishing or identity theft
  • Financial scams or pyramid schemes
  • Sale of counterfeit goods

Illegal Activities

Do not use YAPPZY to promote or engage in illegal activities, including:

  • Sale or promotion of illegal drugs
  • Sale of weapons or regulated goods
  • Human trafficking
  • Copyright or trademark infringement
  • Gambling or unauthorized contests

Intellectual Property

Respect others' intellectual property rights. Only post content you have the right to share.

If you believe content infringes your copyright, report it to copyright@yappzy.online.

Tip: When using music, make sure it's from our licensed library or you have permission to use it.

Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Tap the "..." menu on any post
  • Select "Report"
  • Choose the reason for reporting
  • Submit

Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email us at support@yappzy.online.

Emergency? If you see content involving immediate danger or illegal activity, contact local authorities immediately.

Enforcement

How We Enforce

We use a combination of AI-powered moderation and human review to enforce these guidelines.

Consequences

Depending on the severity and frequency of violations:

  • Warning: For first-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Violating content is removed
  • Temporary Suspension: Account access restricted for a period
  • Permanent Ban: Account permanently deactivated

Appeals

If you believe we made a mistake, you can appeal to support@yappzy.online.
